002 : P-A-Q

Shipping business web application(group project), used MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, Redux, Twilio API, Weather API, HTML5, CSS3

  • Create and update each shipment categorized by the business owner’s ID, and can also check all the orders by the shipment ID
  • Categorize orders and shipments by “delivered” or “not-delivered” for the business owner’s UE
  • Users can sign up and sign in as a customer, create deliveries and update their personal data
  • Twilio API was used to send text messages to customers once their deliveries have been made
  • Weather API was administered to notify the customers of the current weather in Mexico city and Minneapolis
